

How the GIFS are Made

Edit the image in IMovie with Spin In at the  beginning and Spin Out at the end.
(Automatically Turns it into a video) 

Use https://www.unscreen.com to Remove the Black Background (Automatically converts file into GIF)

Use https://redketchup.io/gif-resizer To Resize the Image into 230 x 350 Pixels

Rename and Put into special_gifs file.

Naming Convention = "col_num.gif" where col = Character representing color of card and num = Int representing Type of Card

Has The GIFs for each Special Card and in Each Color
